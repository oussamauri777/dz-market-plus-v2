## Screenshots to Take (on phone after verification)

Open the app and screenshot these screens:

1. **Home screen** - showing the feed of listings
2. **Listing detail** - a product with photos and description
3. **Chat screen** - conversation with a seller
4. **Notifications screen** - bell icon with notifications list
5. **Profile screen** - user profile with settings
6. **Search with filters** - showing AI search or filter drawer
7. **Favorites screen** - saved listings
8. **Categories view** - grid of categories

Requirements:
- PNG or JPEG, 16:9 or 9:16 ratio
- Min 320px, max 3840px on each side
- No text overlays (Google adds those)
